ABOUT US

OUR STORY

We founded Safe Dollar Studio in July 2024 in Johannesburg, South Africa, after a campus game jam where our collaboration earned an honourable mention. That early recognition inspired us to keep creating together and led to a mentorship with TwoNameGames, who guided us in turning our shared passion into an official indie game studio. By March 2025, our team had grown to six dedicated members, and a few months later — in July 2025 — we were honoured to win Best Game at another campus game jam. Today, we're in what we call our "Safe Dollar Season" — a creative challenge where we develop a new game prototype every two weeks. Through this process, we continue to explore new ideas, push our creativity, and deliver playful, innovative experiences to the gaming community.

OUR MISSION & VALUES

Our mission is to create playful, engaging games that spark joy and curiosity, bringing imaginative experiences to players of all ages.

  • Collaboration: We work as a united team, supporting each other to produce quality work.
  • Adaptability: We embrace feedback and guidance with openness, always ready to refine our ideas or let go of what doesn't serve the project.
  • Ambition: We are driven to grow within the industry, striving to improve with each game we create.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated not only to our projects but also to the broader creative community, viewing our journey as part of something larger than ourselves.

HONOURABLE MENTION

In July 2024, we took part in a 3-day campus game jam, where our team received an Honourable Mention for our game Lab Rumble. This event marked the beginning of our journey — it was the first time we worked together as a group, and it ultimately led to the creation of Safe Dollar Studio.

Lab Rumble is a 2D, projectile-based brawler where players must fight for survival on a dying Earth. By picking up and launching items scattered around a chaotic lab, players battle each other for the last chance to escape. The one who survives wins the game.

BEST GAME

In July 2025, we participated in a 3-day campus game jam and proudly received the Best Game Award for our project Mage Brothers. This achievement came just a year after our first award, marking another milestone in our studio's growth and creativity.

Mage Brothers is a fast-paced 2-player wizard shooter, where players duel using a mix of unique attack and support spells. But beware — every spell has its own twist! Each cast comes with unexpected side effects that can impact both your opponent and yourself, making every match unpredictable and exciting.
